Advantage of technology

Development has been a major pursuit of humanity for a long time now. People have tried to improve their ways ofBenefits Of Computer Technology life by innovating numerous and diverse things. One of the greatest advancements of the times has been technology. Technological developments such as computers and locomotives among others have influenced all sectors of human life including health, transport, communication, and education to mention but a few. The developments in the computer technology, in particular, have caused significant changes in the lives of people. The resultant effects of the advancement in computer technology have been either positive or negative to human life. The paper is a research about the computer technology and its effects on human life, particularly in relation to education. The thesis statement of the research is that the advancement of the computer technology has proven to be of a greater good to the human race in the delivery and access of educational services. The research question is: to what extent has the advancement in computer technology been beneficial to humanity in the education sector? Firstly, the paper discusses the various ways in which the computer technology has advanced and the various educational areas of human life it has influenced positively. Secondly, it discusses the negative educational effects of the computer technology on humanity. Lastly, the development experienced in the computer technology has had significant contribution on humanity.

Advantage?

Computer technology is a strong development in the education industry, which is very crucial. It has made it simpler, more convenient and cheaper to access and provide educational services. It also structured and shielded the documents and data management section more than before.

Disadvantage of technology

If businesses want to adapt to the recent technological breakthroughs, they need to allocate resources to upgrade and maintain their infrastructure. Technology is always improving, requiring expensive cycles of upgrading. Companies also need to spend training to acclimatize staff to the changes.

Technology prevalence in companies also invites hazards associated with cybercrime. According to a research released by McAfee in June 2014, companies around the world lose nearly $400 billion annually owing to hacking incidents, theft of intellectual property, credit card fraud and other computer crimes. Businesses must spend resources not only as a precautionary measure to secure their systems, but also for recovery after a cybercrime.

Disadvantage?

Technology has made us completely dependent upon it. Though there is advancement in technology, there is also less security. Maintenance cost is also high because of constantly evolving environment.
